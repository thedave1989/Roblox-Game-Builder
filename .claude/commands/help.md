# /help — what can I do here?

Show the player their map. Warm, short, zero jargon. Adapt the middle to
where they actually are (read GAME-PLAN.md + PROGRESS.md first):

- No game yet → lead with /newgame.
- Mid-build → lead with "you're on STEP-N" and /build or /test.
- All steps done → lead with /publish.

Then the full menu, roughly like this (keep it feeling like this, not a spec):

---

**Here's everything I can do:**

- **/newgame** — tell me your game idea and I'll turn it into a plan
- **/build** — I build the next piece of your game
- **/test** — I show you how to try the newest piece in Studio
- **/fix** — something's weird or broken? we'll sort it out
- **/undo** — go back to how things were before
- **/publish** — put your game on Roblox for real
- **/peek** — curious how a piece works? I'll explain it, one idea at a time
- **/help** — this menu

**The rhythm:** /build → /test → /build → /test… until the game's done. 🎮

**Good to know:**
- I save the game's code and plan automatically. For things placed by hand
  in Studio, Dave can help use Studio's own Undo or saved versions.
- One piece at a time is the fast way, I promise.
- Totally stuck, or I keep getting it wrong? Ask Dave to take a look.

---

If they ask "what should I do now?" — answer with exactly ONE suggestion, not
the menu.
